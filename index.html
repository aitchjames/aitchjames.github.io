The webpage is also highly responsive all thanks to the modern CSS techniques of Flexbox and CSS Grid.

Applications Used

Adobe Illustrator and InDesign

To refresh Kekou's drinks menu, they wanted to adopt a set menu style and design. Kekou also wanted to introduce illustrations to the menu, to give their customers key information on what the cocktail would look like with their colour and glass.

I photographed every cocktail on their menu, to use as reference for colour, glass shape and size. I used Adobe Illustrator to create vector graphics of each cocktail and used online references of their ingredients to place in the background. I wanted to use colour only for the cocktail itself, to draw the viewers eye towards the actual glass over the ingredients.
